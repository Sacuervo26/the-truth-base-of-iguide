Missing a space that can be Drafted Within the Boundary of the Property (Eg. Closets).

This type of error is when a drafter misses drafting something within the property that there is
clear visual and laser data for. More often than not we commonly see this error for missed
closets. Typically closets that contain mirrored sliding doors in bedrooms or hallway linen
closets. Though these internal spaces do not affect the total Interior Area, they affect the
interior layout and division of spaces.
Other spaces that tend to get overlooked are voids or structural voids. Some are hard to find
as they exist within the closet.

Missing Draftable Spaces Error Tolerances
There is no tolerance. If you have to add in the draftable space then you must attribute a level
2 error towards the Drafter. Note down the error on the notepad in Draft and increase the tally
by 1 per instance.
1. Good Example Notes:
a. L2 - The closet bedroom was missing from the floor plan as seen by scan #004.
b. L2 - Pantry not drafted as seen by scan #003.
c. L2 - The excluded Attic is missing the restricted height indications. Scans 012 and
014
2. Bad Example Notes:
a. Missed Closet.
b. Missed Pantry.
c. Missed Ledge.
Take a look at how much detail is provided for the “Good Example”. The location of the
problem/issue should be explained using the floor it is on, the pano/scans that can see it and
what room it’s contained in or next to. We must be descriptive by describing the issue and if
applicable, provide a solution or tips as to how to avoid it in future. The “Bad Example”
provides no details for the Drafter and is completely useless. Providing “Bad Example” notes
will not be tolerated.
*Note: The intent of the error tolerance is to ensure we are not overloading every Drafter with
several minuscule errors. This does not mean we do not correct the error in question. We
always correct everything whether it is within or outside of the error tolerance. The error
tolerance in this document is to be strictly adhered to, making up your own standards or
tolerances will not be permitted.
