Courtesy Update
A Courtesy Update is a task or work order where the operator is requesting a change
that takes little to no effort, resulting in a non-billable task. A courtesy update, unlike
any other updates, is free of charge. These kinds of requests can range but are most
commonly re-dividing spaces or combining spaces such as combining a Living Room &
Dining Room.
Courtesy Updates unfortunately use Full/Original Data only. Light Data is not and
cannot be used to perform a Draft Update.
*This is on R&D’s Roadmap to allow us to use Light Data.

*Note: Most Courtesy Updates start as a “Draft Update” but are editable to be
converted into a “Courtesy Update” at the discretion of the Quality Technician.

Courtesy Update Procedure
The procedure at a glance is to download the published iGUIDE, review the operators
request and/or images and proceed with the changes if applicable. If the changes are
very simple, requiring not a lot of effort on our end, we change the Draft Update to a
Courtesy Update.
1) While in the QA Queue, select the blue “claim next work order” button.

Figure #2 - Claim Next Work Order

2) The Task Details Page will open with the Quality Technician as the claimer listed
under the QA section. No name will be listed under the Drafter section as there
is no Draft to QA workflow for updates.
a) The Description field will indicate what is involved with the update.
*Note: Some descriptions are very thorough, others are not so some investigation
may be required.
3) Review any images provided in the “Supporting Documents” section to help
better understand/supplement the request. Some requests may or may not
have Supporting Documents.

6) Once the project is open, investigate the area/work involved with what the
operator requested. If the request can be accommodated, proceed with
performing the update.
*Note: “If the request can be accommodated” means that not all operator requests
can actually be fulfilled. Some operator requests may be unclear and require us to
contact the Support Team to get in touch with the Operator. Some operator requests
may be something they can actually do themselves on the Portal and we can reject the
task. Some operator requests are above and beyond what we can reasonably do today
and we can reject the task. See associated SOP for more details.

8) Click the verify button to ensure there are no errors. Mitigate any pending
warnings if applicable.
9) Save, Export then close the project.
10) Returning to the Task Details Page, at the top of the page underneath the
Update Badge is a blue “Edit” button. If the “Draft Update” was very simplistic in
nature, requiring little to no effort to complete, we can adjust this update to a
“Courtesy Update” meaning it will be free of charge.
