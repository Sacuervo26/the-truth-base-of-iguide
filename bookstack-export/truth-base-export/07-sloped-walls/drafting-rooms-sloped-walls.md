Drafting Rooms With Sloped Walls/Ceilings
In certain rooms on the upper floor walls may be sloped. If the laser reading is on the sloped
portion of the wall when the camera is set to 5 ft, this reading will not accurately represent the
full size of the room. In these cases follow the steps below,
●

Find a vertical wall reading to use as a baseline and draft using normal techniques.

●

Using the vertical wall as a baseline, set the floor level by middle-clicking where the
vertical wall meets the floor in the Pano Stripe Viewer.

●

Draft a piece of wall to an arbitrary length, then click and drag the wall. This will show
the wall as a yellow dashed outline of where it actually exists along the floor level in the
Pano Stripe Viewer.
