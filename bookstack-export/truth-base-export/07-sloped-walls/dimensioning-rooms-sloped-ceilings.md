Dimensioning Rooms With Sloped Ceilings
If the room is a finished, heated livable space, manual adjustments of the dimension arrows
will need to be made such that they show the full dimension of the room. Manually adjusting
dimension arrows are as follows:
●

Press “R” to enter R MODE.

●

Click+drag on the desired arrow.

●

Hold “ctrl” and drag the arrow to the desired wall.

●

Ensure the arrows snap to the edge of the wall and do not overlap.

●

Where applicable, keep dimension arrows along the same plane (Horizontal, vertical).
