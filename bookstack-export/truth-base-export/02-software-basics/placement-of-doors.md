Placing Doors
Below is an overview of how to place doors and how to identify door types.
●

Press “Q” to open door placement mode.

●

Choose the corresponding door type for each door from the available options.

●

Place the door in its corresponding location.

●

Press “Shift + mousewheel” to adjust the length of the door or use arrows on the door
segment to manually adjust.

●

Use “V” and “H” to adjust the swing and hinge side of the door.
