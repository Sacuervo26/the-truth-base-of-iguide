Other Labels (Manual Labels)
Sometimes a space requires a label with no dimensions or room area associated with it. A
manual label can be created in this case by pressing “L” to enter label mode and then double
left-click to open a text box.
Most common manual labels will be “F/P” for fireplaces, “FOYER” or “ENTRY” for the main
entrance if there isn’t a room created.

When drawing stairs an “UP” or “DN” label is created and it can be manipulated just like other
manual labels. These labels are not affected by the “Autoplace all room labels function”.
Right-click on the manual label and choose Auto-fix all labels and cross-arrows.
●

Ensure all labels are properly rotated such that all text is horizontal (reads left to right)
or vertical (reads bottom to top) if the space is too small to place the label horizontally.
This will occur when the floor is rotated after the label is placed.
