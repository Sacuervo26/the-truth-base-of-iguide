Drafting Exterior Walls
When drafting exterior walls choose a clear, visible, exterior corner to begin drafting the
perimeter.
1. Press “D” to enter draw mode.
2. Press “1” to select exterior wall types.
3. Hold “shift” to show the draft aid.
4. Align the projected lines such that they run as close as possible to the laser data
readings.
5. Left-click to begin the line segment.
6. Trace the exterior of the property.
