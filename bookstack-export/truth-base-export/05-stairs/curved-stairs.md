Drafting Curved Stairs
Curved or helical staircases lead upstairs or downstairs in the course of an arc. These
staircases can be used for the main staircase or as an architectural feature of a residential
house. These types of staircases typically do not contain a central column or support, making it
differ from a spiral staircase.
To draft a curved staircase follow the steps below:
●

Press “D” to enter draw mode.

●

Press “7” to use construction lines.

●

Draw structure lines (D7) overlapping curved walls as accurately as possible by using
convert to curve or by clicking the construction line while holding shift to manipulate
the line into shape.

●

Right-click on the structure line at the start of the stair segment.

●

Hover over Generate Stairs and select either up or down depending on the direction
of stairs.
