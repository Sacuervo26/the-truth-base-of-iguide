Exporting  
The final step in drafting is to create an export file.   
 
● Click the Export button in the toolbar or press “X” to begin exporting. 
● If the initial scan is not already selected by the operator or photographer an orange 
square will appear in the Pano Stripe Viewer. This square represents the initial view 
for the virtual tour.  
○ Choose a view from the main floor that shows a nice view of the property. 
● If there are any outstanding errors or warnings a prompt will appear. This will 
summarize and outline any warning or error that may have been missed during the 
verify step.  
○ Resolve these issues in case they were missed or click the ignore button if the 
warnings or errors are ignorable.   
○ In most cases, selecting the issue in the warning window will move the drafting 
environment to the location of the issue and zoom. 
● Wait for the export process to complete.
