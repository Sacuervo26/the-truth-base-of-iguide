Drafting Curved Walls
Certain properties may contain curved walls in both interior and exterior walls. The method to
draft these curved walls is identical in both situations.
1. Draft a straight wall segment between the two endpoints of the curve.
2. Right-click this line segment while in E mode, select either convert to curve or convert
to semi-circle.
If the initial curve is sloped along the wrong axis, pressing “V” will flip the direction of the curve.
The shape of the curve can be further manipulated after it is created by holding shift + left click
and dragging on various points along the curve to best match the laser data.
